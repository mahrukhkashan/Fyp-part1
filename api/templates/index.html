Key Features

Advanced AI capabilities for comprehensive sepsis management

AI Prediction

Machine learning models analyze patient data to predict sepsis risk with high accuracy using both structured and unstructured data.

  • Real-time risk assessment
  • Multimodal data integration
  • Continuous monitoring

Explainable AI

Understand why predictions are made with SHAP-based explanations and visualizations that show feature importance.

  • Transparent decision-making
  • Feature importance ranking
  • Clinical justification

AI Assistant

Interactive chatbot provides explanations, answers questions, and guides users through prevention and treatment information.

  • 24/7 patient support
  • Multilingual capabilities
  • Personalized guidance

About This Project

This Explainable Multimodal AI-based Sepsis Prediction and Assistance System is developed as a Final Year Project by Computer Science students at University of Engineering and Technology Lahore.

Mission

To reduce sepsis mortality through early detection and transparent AI explanations.

Vision

Make AI-driven healthcare accessible and understandable for everyone.

Why Sepsis Prediction Matters
The Problem
  • Sepsis causes 20% of all global deaths
  • Early detection reduces mortality by 50%
  • Every hour of delay increases risk by 8%
Our Solution
  • AI predicts sepsis 6-12 hours earlier
  • Provides clear explanations for trust
  • Accessible to both clinicians and patients
Impact
  • Reduces false alarms by 30%
  • Improves clinician decision-making
  • Empowers patients with knowledge
